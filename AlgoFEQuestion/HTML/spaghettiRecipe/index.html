Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti

Instructions

  2. Add Spaghetti to boil water.
    1. Add salt to tast.
    2. Coke for 10 mintue
  3. Add Marinara Sauce to pan.
  4. Mixed cooked Spaghetti with Marinara Sauce.
  5. Enjoy!
